Suggest meals from default items list of Duke.
Various optional parameters exist to customize the suggestions.

    suggest [/date] <DATE> [/type] <MealType> [/display] <Integer>

For example:

1. You want a dinner meal suggestion for 29/10/2019 with up to maximum 5 meals to be suggested.

    suggest /date 29/10/2019 /type d /display 5

2. You want a breakfast suggestion for today.

    suggest /type b
