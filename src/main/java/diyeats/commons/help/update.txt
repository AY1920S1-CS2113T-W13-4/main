update the user profile

    update [/TAG]<VALUE>...

Tags you can use are: /name /age /weight /date /height /activity

For example:

1. You want to update your body weight for the day to 50 kg

    update /weight 50

2. You want to update your body weight on 10/11/2019 to 52 kg

    update /weight 52 /date 10/11/2019
